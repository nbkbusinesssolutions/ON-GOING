Booking & Planning

Confirm your ride in minutes via WhatsApp or call.

How do I book a car?
Text us on WhatsApp at +91 79727 19764 with your travel dates and preferred car. We confirm availability within minutes, send a secure payment link, and block your car once payment is received. Prefer calling? That works too.
How far in advance should I book?
Peak season (Dec–Jan): Reserve 5–7 days ahead.
Off-season / weekdays: Same-day bookings are often available.
Pro tip: Thar and Crysta models book out fastest—lock them early.
Can I book and pick up today?
Yes! Message us, we’ll check availability, process payment online, and have your keys ready at our Madgaon hub within 60 minutes.

Pricing & Deposits

Clear breakdowns with unlimited kilometres included.

What are your daily rental rates?
  • Swift / Ignis: ₹1,200 per day
  • Baleno / i20: ₹1,300 per day
  • Ertiga / Brezza: ₹1,800–₹2,000 per day
  • Thar / Crysta: ₹3,000 per day
Includes unlimited kilometres and comprehensive insurance.
Is there a security deposit?
Yes, ₹3,000 refundable deposit. Returned instantly via UPI/bank transfer once you return the car on time with no major damage.
Any hidden charges?
Zero. You pay only:
  1. Daily rental rate
  2. ₹3,000 refundable deposit
  3. Fuel consumed (return at same level)
  4. Optional doorstep delivery: ₹300–₹500
Payment methods: UPI, bank transfer, or cash at pickup.

Cars & Usage

Know what’s included before you hit the road.

Is fuel included?
Fuel is pay-as-you-go. We note the fuel level at handover—simply refill to the same level before returning.
Any kilometer limits?
None. All rentals include unlimited kilometers within Goa. Outside-Goa travel is not permitted.
Is insurance included?
Every car comes with comprehensive insurance and 24/7 roadside assistance. In case of accidents, you’re responsible only for the ₹3,000 deductible. Maximum speed limit: 80 km/h within Goa for compliance.
📄

Documents & Eligibility

Bring the essentials and you’re good to go.

What documents do I need?
  • Original valid driving license (permanent, not learner’s)
  • Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport)
Verification takes under 2 minutes at pickup.
Minimum age requirements?
Standard cars: 21 years. Thar/Crysta: 23 years. Learner’s licenses are not accepted.
What about foreign licenses?
International travellers need an International Driving Permit (IDP) plus a passport copy.
What if my ID and license don't match?
Names must match across documents. Address differences are acceptable. Send photos via WhatsApp for pre-verification if you’re unsure.

Pickup & Drop

Choose the handover style that fits your schedule.

Where do I collect my car?
Red Roof Hotel, Madel — 2 minutes from KTC Bus Stand, Madgaon.
Doorstep delivery available?
Yes! We deliver anywhere in South Goa for ₹300–₹500 depending on distance.
Pickup/drop timings?
Standard hours: 8 AM – 8 PM daily. Late-night or early-morning slots are available on request (additional charges may apply).
Where do I return the car?
Return it to Red Roof Hotel, Madel. If we delivered it to you, we can collect from the same location for the delivery fee.

Safety & Rules

Drive confidently knowing we’ve got your back.

What happens if I have an accident?
Call us immediately. File a police report if required. Insurance covers damages beyond the ₹3,000 deductible. We handle towing, assistance, and all paperwork.
Who pays for traffic fines?
Any fines incurred during your rental are your responsibility. Pay directly or we deduct from the deposit (with proof).
Can I smoke or drink in the car?
❌ Smoking not allowed (keeps cars fresh for everyone).
❌ Drinking & driving strictly prohibited—violations result in immediate rental termination and deposit forfeiture.
